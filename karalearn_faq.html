Frequently Asked Questions

What does KaraLearn do?

KaraLearn is an innovative app designed to make learning Chinese fun and engaging through music. It combines the joy of listening to music with the educational benefit of language learning, allowing users to learn Chinese doing exercises on famous mandopop songs, adapted to the learner's skills. \n\nThe app provides fun exercises with the song lyrics, as well as a 'study' mode where users can check the lyrics of their favorite song, along with their translations and pinyin translitteration, enabling users to improve their language skills while enjoying their favorite tunes.

What is "HSK" exactly? I see it everywhere in the app.

The HSK (汉语水平考试, Hanyu Shuiping Kaoshi) is the official Chinese language proficiency test for non-native speakers, administered by Hanban. It's divided into six levels, from basic (HSK 1-2) to advanced (HSK 5-6), testing listening, reading, and writing skills (writing starts from HSK 3). The test is globally recognized and often used for educational and professional purposes, such as university admissions in China. The HSK certificates are permanently valid, though recent ones are preferred in some contexts. \n\nKaralearn uses HSK as a standard for measuring and organizing the difficulty of the lyrics, so that if you're studing to prepare for an HSK exam, you can use this app to practice the HSK words for your exam.
